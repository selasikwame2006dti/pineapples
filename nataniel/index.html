Our Story

Sustainable Pineapple Farming Since 2005

Founded by Nathaniel Asante, our farm has been dedicated to producing the highest quality pineapples using sustainable farming practices. Located in the fertile coastal region of Ghana, we combine traditional knowledge with modern agricultural techniques.

Our commitment to environmental stewardship and community development has made us a trusted partner for both local markets and international exporters.

Our Pineapple Varieties

We cultivate several premium pineapple varieties, each with unique characteristics and flavors

Golden Sweet

Our signature variety with exceptional sweetness and low acidity. Perfect for fresh consumption and juicing.

Queen Victoria

Medium-sized fruit with golden-yellow flesh and excellent flavor balance. Popular for export markets.

GHS 18.00/kg

Smooth Cayenne

Large fruit with pale yellow flesh and mild acidity. Ideal for canning and processing.
